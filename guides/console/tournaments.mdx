# Tournaments

The core of the system.

## Creation Wizard
1.  **General Details**: Name, Description, Game.
2.  **Registration**: Open/Close dates, entry fees.
3.  **Format**: Single/Double Elim, Round Robin.
4.  **Schedule**: Match intervals and round timings.

## Statuses
- **Draft**: Being configured.
- **Published**: Visible to users, open for registration.
- **Seeding**: Registration closed, preparing bracket.
- **Ongoing**: Matches are live.
- **Completed**: Winners declared.
