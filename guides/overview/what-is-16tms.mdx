**16TMS** is a comprehensive Tournament Management System designed to power competitive gaming experiences.

## Our Solution

We offer:
- **Integration**: Seamlessly integrate tournament functionality into your existing games or platforms.
- **White-label Solutions**: A fully branded tournament platform ready for your users.
- **Console**: A powerful dashboard to manage everything from games to disputes.

## Who is it for?

- **Game Developers**: Add competitive layers to your titles.
- **Esports Organizations**: Run professional leagues and cups.
- **Community Managers**: Engage your community with automated events.
